Recovery is where a chief of staff earns their value. The right move is rarely 'send a reminder.' It's understanding what went wrong, what the person responds to, and what specific action has the highest chance of reopening the thread. Sometimes the best recovery is to wait. Sometimes it's to narrow scope. Sometimes it's to involve someone new.
