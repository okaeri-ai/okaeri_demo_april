the principle
okaeri · system design
Okaeri is not a CRM. It's the intelligence layer that makes your CRM useful. Your CRM knows deal stages and contact records. Okaeri knows who actually committed, with what conviction, blocked by what, and whether the deal confidence is rising or falling. The CRM is the system of record. Okaeri is the system of understanding.
