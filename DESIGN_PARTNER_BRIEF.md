# Okaeri — Design Partner Program

## What We're Building

Okaeri is a voice-first execution engine. It listens to your meetings, extracts commitments, and executes them across your tools — with earned trust, not blind automation.

We're looking for 3-5 design partners who run 5+ meetings per week and want to stop losing commitments in the gap between conversation and action.

## What Design Partners Get

### Immediate
- Early access to the production product (ahead of general availability)
- Direct influence on product direction — your feedback shapes what gets built
- Weekly check-ins with the founding team
- Priority support and custom integration work

### Within 30 Days
- Voice-first meeting capture with real-time extraction
- Automatic execution across Linear, Slack, and Google Calendar
- Prep briefs before every meeting with relationship context
- Execution rate tracking and weekly digest

### Within 90 Days
- Earned autonomy model — Okaeri earns the right to act independently
- Meeting steering — real-time structural guidance during calls
- Commitment attribution — who suggested, who committed, who can block
- Change detection across meetings
- Relationship strategy with operator notes

## What We Ask From Partners

### Commitment
- Use Okaeri in at least 5 meetings per week for 90 days
- Provide feedback after each week (15-minute call or async voice note)
- Allow anonymous usage data for product improvement
- Be honest about what doesn't work — corrections make the system better

### Expectations
- The product will have rough edges in the first 2 weeks
- Extraction accuracy starts at ~84% and improves to ~96% by week 4
- Some features will change based on your feedback
- You'll see bugs. Report them, and they'll be fixed fast.

### What We Don't Ask
- No payment during the partner period
- No exclusivity — use whatever other tools you want alongside
- No PR or public endorsement requirement
- No minimum contract after the partner period

## Ideal Partner Profile

**Best fit:**
- Seed to Series A founder or operator
- 5-10 meetings per week (team syncs, investor calls, partner meetings)
- Uses Linear, Slack, and Google Calendar (Salesforce and Gmail support coming)
- Comfortable with voice-first interaction

**Not a fit (yet):**

- Teams that need multi-language support (English-only initially)

## Onboarding Flow

### Week 0: Setup (30 minutes)
1. Connect tools (Linear, Slack, Calendar)
2. Record your name and key relationships
3. Teach Okaeri 3-5 operating rules by voice
4. Run one test meeting to verify extraction

### Week 1: Observer Mode
- Okaeri listens and extracts but does not execute
- You review every extraction and correct errors
- The system builds its initial trust baseline
- Weekly check-in: what worked, what didn't

### Week 2-3: Confirmed Executor
- Okaeri executes after your confirmation on each action
- Trust scores begin accumulating per tool
- Prep briefs activate for recurring meetings
- Feedback: are confirmations annoying or valuable?

### Week 4-8: Trusted Partner
- High-trust tools (Linear, Slack) begin auto-executing
- Meeting steering activates for team meetings
- Relationship strategy surfaces in prep briefs
- Voice signals (conviction, hesitation) inform confidence

### Week 9-12: Full Feature Set
- All features active including contradiction detection, change tracking, and operating cadence automation
- Final feedback session and decision on continued usage
- Discussion of post-partner pricing and terms

## FAQ

**How long is the commitment?**
90 days. You can stop at any time, but the system needs at least 4 weeks to show meaningful accuracy improvement.

**What data do you store?**
Extracted commitments are stored indefinitely. Transcripts are stored for 90 days. Voice recordings are processed and deleted within 24 hours. You can delete everything at any time.

**Can my team use it too?**
Yes. We support up to 8 team members during the partner period. Each person builds their own trust model.

**What happens after 90 days?**
We discuss pricing and ongoing terms. Partners who provided significant feedback get preferred pricing.

**Is there an NDA?**
We'll send a lightweight mutual NDA. Nothing onerous.
